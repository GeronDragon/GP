A Dual Mode Redundant Approach for Microprocessor Soft Error Hardness 微处理器软错误硬度双模冗余方法

A dual mode redundant (DMR) logic data path with instruction restart that detects errors at register file (RF) write-back is presented. 双模式冗余（DMR）的逻辑数据路径与指令重启，在寄存器文件检测到错误（RF）回写呈现。 TheDMRRF allows SEU correction using parity to detect RF entry nibbles that are correct in one copy but not the other. TheDMRRF允许使用奇偶校验检测RF进入半字节是在一个正确副本，而不是其他的SEU校正。 Detection and backing out incorrect write data are also described. 检测和背出不正确的写数据也有所说明。 The radiation hardened by design (RHBD) circuits are implemented in 90 nm CMOS. 设计硬化辐射（RHBD）电路的90纳米CMOS工艺实现。 The DMR microarchitecture is described, including pipelining, error handling, and the associated hardware. 对DMR微架构中描述，包括流水线，错误处理和相关的硬件。 Heavy ion and proton testing validate the approach. 重离子和质子测试验证的方法。 Experimentally measured cross sections and examples of errors due to pipeline SET or RF SEU are shown. 实验测得的横截面和由于管道SET或射频SEU错误的示例被示出。 Critical node spacing and the mitigation of multiple node collection are also described. 临界节点间距和多个节点集合的缓解也有所说明。

I. INTRODUCTION 引言

RADIATION hardening is increasingly important for both commercial terrestrial systems and those aimed at harsh environments, eg, space electronics. 抗辐射加固是为商业地面系统和那些旨在恶劣的环境，如太空电子越来越重要。

  1. Single Event Effects 单粒子效应

Soft errors due to radiation induced charge collection have long been a major concern for memories. 由于辐射引起的电荷收集软错误长期以来一直是记忆的一个主要问题。 It is of increasing concern in scaled VLSI circuits, due to reduced current drive and capacitance at both logic and storage nodes, and relatively constant charge deposition and collection. 它是在规模VLSI电路越来越多的关注，由于减少了电流驱动和电容逻辑和存储节点和相对恒定的电荷沉积和收藏。 These factors combine to create longer single event transients (SETs) and greater likelihood of single event upset (SEU) at storage node latches [1]–[5]. 这些因素结合起来以创建更长单一事件瞬变（套）和单一事件的可能性更大，在存储节点锁存干扰（SEU）[1] - [5]。 Soft errors have become increasingly problematic for terrestrial processing, and remain a severe issue for space based electronics. 软错误已经成为地面处理越来越困难，并保持对空间基础的电子严峻的问题。 Error detection and correction (EDAC) has been used to protect large memories, eg, microprocessor caches for many years, but is ineffective for protecting data path logic pipelines. 误差检测和校正（EDAC）已被用来保护大的存储器，例如，微处理器缓存多年，但是无效的保护数据路径的逻辑管道。 Additionally, SETs can affect control logic, essentially changing the circuit operation from the specified behavior, which may cause inadvertent data or operations in a microprocessor. 此外，组可以影响控制逻辑，基本上从指定的行为，这可能导致在一个微处理器无意数据或操作改变电路的操作。

The two primary methods for hardening logic are redundancy in time (temporal approaches) and redundancy in space (dualmodular and triple-modular redundancy, DMR and TMR, respectively). 两个主要的方法进行硬化的逻辑是冗余的时间（时间的方法）和冗余空间（dualmodular和三模冗余，DMR和TMR，分别）。 Temporal approaches are straightforward, but have the drawback of placing large delays in the timing critical path, essentially increasing the setup time for the circuit, whether accomplished by special process (usually resistor and capacitor) elements [6] or by radiation hardening by design (RHBD) [7], [8]. 颞方法是简单的，但具有将大延迟的定时关键路径，实质上增加了建立时间的电路的缺点，无论是实现通过特殊工艺（通常电阻和电容）的元素[6]或通过设计辐射硬化（ RHBD）[7]，[8]。 The delay must exceed the worst-case single event transient (SET) duration, which for bulk CMOS processes has been measured to exceed 1 ns [2]–[4]. 延迟必须超过最坏情况的单事件瞬态（SET）的持续时间，这对于体CMOS工艺已经测量超过1纳秒[2] - [4]。 Finally, besides the performance impact, many schemes are still susceptible to clock or control logic errors [9], [10]. 最后，除了对性能的影响，许多方案仍然易受时钟或控制逻辑错误[9]，[10]。

TMR has been implemented at multiple granularities, with checking ranging from the sequential element [11] to the board level [12], [13]. TMR已经实现在多个粒度，以检查从顺序元素[11]将板级[12]，[13]。 Both DMR and TMR can achieve clock rates similar to commercial microcircuits, but at the cost of die area and increased power. 既DMR和TMR可以实现时钟速率类似于商业微电路，但在芯片面积和增大的功率的成本。 DMR has a 33% power and area advantage over TMR, but unlike TMR where majority voting can be used for correction, the correct data or control signals cannot be determined readily. DMR有33％的功率和面积优势的TMR，但不同于TMR哪里多数表决可用于校正时，正确的数据或控制信号不能被容易地确定。

  1. Register Files 注册文件

Register files (RFs) are key components in high performance circuits, and especially, microprocessors. 寄存器文件（RF）的是在高性能电路关键部件，特别是，微处理器。 RFs are differentiated from standard SRAM in that the readout is usually single ended, and they have as few as two to dozens of read ports. 射频从标准SRAM中，该读出通常是单端分化，并且它们具有少至两到几十个读端口。 Moreover, their size is much smaller, commonly ranging from 32 to 256 entries. 此外，它们的尺寸要小得多，通常范围从32到256个条目。 In a microprocessor the RF resides in the key critical timing paths of the ALU/bypass loop, where operands must be read from the RF, modified by the ALU, and subsequently used in one clock cycle. 在微处理器中的射频驻留在ALU /旁路循环，在其中的操作数必须从RF读取的密钥关键时序路径，由ALU的改性，并随后在一个时钟周期中使用。

The Alpha 21264 microprocessor used two RFs, each supporting decoupled superscalar data paths [14]. 阿尔法21264微处理器使用两种射频，每个支撑解耦超标量数据通路[14]。 Breaking the RF into two units allowed four (fully differential) readout ports for each data path. 打破了RF到允许四（全差分）读出端口为每个数据路径中的两个单元。 Write back operations in the two RFs were independent, with an extra clock for bypassing results from one register file to another, and no soft error checking mechanism was supported. 回写在两个射频的操作是独立的，具有一个额外的时钟用于旁路从一个寄存器文件的结果到另一个，并且得到了支持无软错误检查机制。

The addition of parity to the 90-nm Itanium RF illustrates the increasing importance of mitigating soft errors in terrestrial ICs, particularly servers [15]. 加校验的90纳米安腾射频说明地面集成电路缓解软错误的重要性日益增加，特别是服务器[15]。 This design adds a serial parity calculation to each register, with four clocks of latency after a write, during which time the register is unprotected. 这种设计增加了一个串行奇偶校验计算每个寄存器，与四个时钟延迟的写入之后，在此期间，寄存器是不受保护的。 Checking is static, but no repair mechanism is provided. 检查是静态的，但没有提供修复机制。 RF errors are thus detectable but not correctable. RF错误从而检测到，但无法纠正。

  1. Register File Soft Error Mitigation 注册文件软错误缓解

A key problem in RF soft error protection is the lack of time in the pipeline, ie, critical timing paths, for EDAC calculation in the stages leading to and from the RF. 在射频软错误保护的一个关键问题是在管道中的时间不够，即，关键时序路径，为EDAC计算在通往和来自RF的各个阶段。 EDAC also requires a 62.5% increase in storage capacity for byte level correction [16], which is closer to DMR than no redundancy. EDAC也需要增加存储容量字节等级校正[16]，这是更接近DMR比没有冗余62.5％。 Wider protection requires deeper parity circuits, adding two to four inversions to the critical timing path. 更广泛的保护需要更深入的奇偶校验电路，增加两到四个反转的关键时序路径。 Consequently, RFs more commonly use parity protection, which is fast to generate, but can only allowerror detection. 因此，射频更通常使用奇偶校验保护，这是快速产生，但只能allowerror检测。 Mohr presented a sufficiently fastXY parity based EDAC scheme to allow its use in RFs [17]. 莫尔提出足够fastXY基于平价EDAC计划，允许其在射频[17]使用。 However, EDAC does not protect against erroneous data or operations caused by SETs, either in the RF or in the ALU/bypass circuitry that produces and consumes data residing in the RF. 然而，EDAC并不防止错误的数据或由其引起的SET操作，要么在RF中或在生产和消费驻留在RF数据的ALU /旁路电路。 Analysis of synthesized RFs on a 130 nm CMOS process showed that TMR had similar area impact as adding EDAC, while the latter approximately doubled the access time [18]. 在130纳米CMOS工艺合成射频的分析表明，TMR具有相似的面积的影响，因为加入EDAC，而后者近似加倍存取时间[18]。 FPGA synthesized RF analysis shows greater TMR area and performance impact, but better hardness [19]. FPGA RF合成分析显示出更大的TMR的面积和性能的影响，但较好的硬度[19]。

II. II。 REGISTER FILE CIRCUITS AND MICROARCHITECTURE 寄存器文件，电路与微架构

The RF design here combines multiple microarchitectural and circuit level approaches, starting with DMR for compatibility with the data path. 射频设计这里将多个微结构和电路级的方法，从DMR用于与数据路径的兼容性。 DMR decoders generate redundant read and write word line signals. DMR解码器产生冗余读取和写入字线信号。 Full SEE protection is provided by DMR combined with large critical node separation through bit interleaving and parity. 全SEE保护由DMR结合，通过比特交织和奇偶大型关键节点的分离提供。 Parity provides error detection; 奇偶校验提供错误检测; DMR allows one copy to provide clean data for SEU corrections; DMR允许一个副本提供干净的数据为SEU改正; interleaving prevents multi-cell upsets (MCUs) from causing uncorrectable errors; 交织防止多小区翻倒控制器（MCU）从导致不可校正的错误; inadvertent operations are prevented by circuit approaches. 意外的操作是由电路的方法预防。 The parity protection is nibble based. 奇偶校验保护是基于蚕食。 This allows the greatest possible critical node spacing, albeit at the expense of twice the parity storage, but with the advantage of one less XOR gate in the parity generation and checking paths. 这允许最大可能的关键节点间隔，尽管在奇偶校验存储两倍的费用，但与在奇偶生成少一个异或门，并检查路径的优点。

Astandard load-store CPUwould have two read ports and one write port, supporting the ALU operands and results, respectively. Astandard加载-存储CPUwould有两个读端口和一个写端口，支持的ALU操作数和结果分别。 Referring to Fig. 参照图 1, this design adds a third, ie, the Rt/Rd read port used to read a copy of the destination register contents about to be overwritten in the next cycle WB stage. 1，这样的设计增加了第三，即保留时间/路读端口用于读取的关于在下一周期WB阶段被覆盖的目标寄存器内容的副本。 Thus, the overwritten RF data can be restored if the overwriting RF store is cancelled due to a detected data path error (SEU or SET) which can be detected as the WB is in progress. 因此，如果重写射频店由于检测到的数据路径错误（SEU或设置），它可以被检测为WB正在进行取消了覆盖RF数据可以被恢复。 Since DMR does not indicate which copy is in error, the original data are returned to the RF and then the instruction with non-matching results is restarted from the beginning of the pipeline. 因为DMR不指示哪个复制是错误的，原始数据被返回到射频，然后与非匹配结果的指令从流水线的开头重新启动。

Finally, since the RF state is unknown at power up, the processor control logic tracks whether a cell has been written, ie, if the parity is valid. 最后，由于在RF状态是未知的，在通电时，处理器的控制逻辑追踪的小区是否已经被写入，也就是说，如果奇偶校验是有效的。 This TMR logic validates parity checking on an entry by entry basis. 这TMR逻辑验证奇偶校验由入门基础条目。

  1. Error Detection and Operation 错误检测和操作

As mentioned, in the target microprocessor, all data path actions that update the RF check the DMR data at the RF. 如所提到的，在目标微处理器，即更新该射频的所有数据路径操作检查DMR数据在RF。 TheWB occurs, and in the event that the A and B copy data do not match, a detected SEE results in an exception in the actual processor. TheWB发生，并且在事件的A和B复制的数据不匹配时，检测到的SEE导致在实际处理器的异常。 Note that there is insufficient time to avoid a write, but the machine is stalled to keep the incorrect state from propagating further into the architectural state—the erroneously written value is backed out. 请注意，没有足够的时间，以避免写的，但机器停转，保持不正确的状态进一步传播到结构状态，错误地写入值回退。

To allow this, a specific exception type, ie, the SEE exception, is added for detected soft errors. 为了实现这一点，一个特定的异常类型，即请参见异常，增加了对检测到软错误。 An added instruction, which when executed (within the SEE exception handler) replaces the values that may have been corrupted, is the back up register file (BURF) instruction. 一个额外的指令，其中，当（在SEE异常处理程序）执行替换可能已损坏的值，是在备份寄存器文件（BURF）指令。 The BURF instruction replaces the RF entry that was last written with the value previously read out of the Rt/Rd port. 该BURF指令取代了RF进入最后写入先前读出RT /路端口值。 Many different types of soft errors may result in a soft error exception, and since the replacement operation should only occur if the last instruction wrote the RF, a processor flag is set on each WB and reset on each non-WB operation. 许多不同类型的软错误可能导致软错误异常，并且如果最后一条指令写在RF重置每个非WB操作，因为替换操作应该只发生，处理器标志被设置在每个WB和。 This flag gates the BURF execution. 此标志城门BURF执行。 In the test chip used for experimental validation, the RF backup is handled by an equivalent TMR test engine instruction, but the action is the same. 在用于实验验证的测试芯片，射频备份由一个等效的TMR测试引擎指令处理的，但动作是相同的。

In Fig. 图。 1, the checking circuits are evident in the block labeled 'A vs. B data comparison', which checks that the WB data match and thus is not in error. 1，支票电路是显而易见的，在标有“A与B数据比较'块，检查了世行数据匹配，因此不能出错。 The same WB checking mechanism is used to detect write word line (WWL) mismatch errors that occur when one of the two WWL copies is asserted, but not the other. 相同的WB检查机制被用于检测当两个WWL副本中的一个被认定时发生写入字线（WWL）失配误差，但不是其他。 Also, the saved value read from the Rt/Rd port is implemented with fine grained self-correcting logic, like the test engine. 此外，从保留时间/路端口读取所保存的值与细粒自校正逻辑来实现，如测试引擎。

For example, when the detector circuit determines only one WWL is asserted on a write operation, the write also fails. 例如，当检测器电路确定只有一个WWL被断言在一个写操作中，写也将失败。 The pipeline is then stalled to avoid propagating incorrect state. 这条管道，然后停顿，以避免传播不正确的状态。 Then the prior value in the register entry intended to be written is restored— this restores the correct prior RF architectural state. 然后在旨在被写入寄存器条目的现有值是restored-这个恢复正确现有射频架构状态。 The operation where the error was detected is subsequently restarted with correct data. 检测到错误的操作是随后重新开始与正确的数据。 Key recovery state is stored in self-correcting TMR circuits [11]. 密钥恢复状态存储在自我修正的TMR电路[11]。 Similarly, if one of the WWLs is truncated, which may partially write both copies, the entire operation is started over from the previously correct architectural state. 类似地，如果WWLs之一被截断，这可能部分地写两个副本，从先前正确的架构状态开始在整个操作。 Additionally, in the case where the A and B data do not match, the corruption could have been from any control or data path logic SET or latch SEU. 此外，在A和B的数据不匹配的情况下，腐败可能已经从任何控制或数据通道逻辑SET或闭锁SEU。 Restarting these operations protects this logic. 重新启动这些行动保护这个逻辑。 Soft errors manifest as read word line (RWL) errors, if they affect the data read, are corrected similarly. 表现为读字线软错误（RWL）的错误，如果他们影响数据读取，同样被纠正。

  1. Register File SEU Recovery 注册文件SEU恢复

The RF circuits support new processor instructions where parity groups in RF copy A with correct parity overwrite the version in copy B, and vice versa, in two clock cycles. 该射频电路支持，其中射频奇偶校验组复制与正确的校验覆盖副本B，反之亦然版本，在两个时钟周期新的处理器指令。 In the target processor, an added processor instruction activates this hardware, ie, repair general purpose register (RGPR), under software control. 在目标处理器，一个附加处理器指令激活这个硬件，即，修通用寄存器（RGPR），在软件控制下。 To keep the hardware simple, no hardware interlocks are implemented—NOP instructions are required to provide a second cycle for the repair. 为了保持硬件简单，没有硬件联锁实现-NOP指令都需要提供第二个周期的修为。 In this manner, potentially SEU corrupted RF state can be repaired in 64 clock cycles (the time to read each register and then write it back) when an error is detected. 以这种方式，有可能的SEU损坏射频状态可以在64个时钟周期中检测出错误时（读取每个寄存器，然后写回的时间）进行修复。 The control logic is very simple—only when the A parity is incorrect is the A copy overwritten, otherwise the B copy is.   1. Analysis 分析

Only one RF port is used in the static tests, and thus the RBL can only upset when the data would have retained the RBL high. 仅一个RF端口用于在静态测试，并因此RBL只能翻倒时数据将都保留了RBL高。 Consequently, in ion testing over 98% of the SETs were detected with logic '1' stored, leading us to conclude that the dynamic RBLs dominate the RF SET cross section (see Fig. 8(b)). In proton testing SETs were only detected with logic '1' in the cell. 因此，在离子测试套超过98％的有逻辑检测'1'存储，导致我们得出结论，动态RBLs的主宰RF SET横截面（参见图8（B）），在质子测试集显只与小区逻辑'1'检测到。 The time to dump the RF state after a dynamic error was detected was 13.7 s. 倾倒检测到动态错误之后的RF状态的时间为13.7秒。 This may have also allowed some additional error accumulation (reported as SEU in Figs. 10 and 11). 这可能也允许一些额外的误差累积（在图10和11报告为SEU）。

The data confirm that the proposed DMR hardening techniques will be effective. 数据证实，所提出的DMR加强技术将是有效的。 SEU MCU extent was far below the critical node spacing. SEU单片机程度远低于临界节点间距。 While many bit errors (representing a potentially large equivalent MCU extent) were detected in the dynamic tests, the many bit errors occurred only in one, eg, the A but not B, copy. 虽然许多位错误（代表一个潜在的大当量MCU程度）在动态测试中进行检测，只发生在一个，例如，在A，但不是B，复制许多位错误。 Regardless, these operations are restarted. 无论如何，这些操作是重新启动。 Uncorrectable errors were noted in some proton tests at high flux. 不可纠正的错误，指出在高通量一些质子测试。 Similar errors were produced in heavy ion testing when the PLL was not shielded, but not with the PLL shield properly positioned, and were thus attributed to clock upsets, which cause setup time failures. 类似的错误产生于重离子检测当PLL没有屏蔽，但不与PLL屏蔽正确定位，并由此归因于时钟失常，导致建立时间故障。 We relied on the non-redundant bypass mechanism within the foundry IP PLL, so could not determine if the errors were due to clock SETs or error accumulation. 我们依赖于铸造的IP PLL非冗余旁通机构，所以不能确定是否错误是由于时钟集或误差积累。

IV. IV。 SUMMARY AND CONCLUSIONS 总结和结论

This paper has described a DMR approach to microprocessor data path and RF hardening that is capable of commercial microprocessor speeds. 本文描述了一个DMR方法微处理器数据路径和射频硬化，能够商用微处理器的速度。 Error detection is simple as applied only at RF write back and during scrub operations prior to WB, and is capable of mitigating any data path SETs, including those on control logic. 应用于只在RF写回，并在之前的世行擦洗操作错误检测是简单，并且能够减轻任何数据路径集，包括那些在控制逻辑。

While the design presented here has both DMR data path and RF logic, duplicating only the latter is applicable to commercial designs. 而这里介绍的设计有两个DMR数据路径和射频逻辑，复制只有后者是适用于商业的设计。 Lack of DMR data path and control signals removes the SET protection, but provides the SEU protection when joined to the parity based repair mechanism, with minimal timing impact. 缺乏DMR数据路径和控制信号的删除集保护，但是当连接到基于奇偶修复机制，以最小的定时冲击提供SEU保护。
